Electronics Design

PCB Designing

For this assignment, I decided to use Fusion to design the PCB.

First, I created the schematics for the PCB, adding the pin headers, button, led, and resistor.

Next, I switched to the PCB view and routed the components together, making sure that there were no violations.

Finally, I turned off all the layers, leaving only the board trace and outline.

For the milling and soldering, they were documented under Electronics Production.
